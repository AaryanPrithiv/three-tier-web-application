1) Access the application

- If you enabled Ingress, configure your local /etc/hosts or DNS to point the hostname to your ingress controller.
- Or port-forward the frontend service:

  kubectl -n {{ .Release.Namespace }} port-forward svc/{{ include "threeTier.fullname" . }}-frontend 8080:{{ .Values.frontend.service.port }}

Then open: http://localhost:8080

2) Backend health endpoint

  kubectl -n {{ .Release.Namespace }} port-forward svc/{{ include "threeTier.fullname" . }}-backend 5000:{{ .Values.backend.service.port }}
  curl -sS http://localhost:5000/api/healthz
